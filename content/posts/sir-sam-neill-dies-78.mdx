## What happened
Sir Sam Neill died on July 13, 2026, at St Vincent’s Private Hospital in Sydney, with his family by his side. The announcement, posted by his whānau, emphasized his dignity and the privacy they request during this immeasurable loss.

Born Nigel John Dermot Neill in 1947, he moved to Christchurch in 1954 and adopted the name Sam while at school. After studying at Canterbury University, he launched his acting career in the early 1970s, breaking out with 1977’s *Sleeping Dogs* and gaining international attention with *My Brilliant Career* (1979) and *Omen III* (1981). His most iconic role came as Dr. Alan Grant in *Jurassic Park* (1993), cementing his status as a global star.

In March 2023, Neill disclosed a diagnosis of stage‑three angioimmunoblastic T‑cell lymphoma, undergoing monthly chemotherapy until remission. At the time of his death, he was cancer‑free, a fact highlighted by his family’s statement.
