## What happened
The Round of 16 saw several high-stakes matches determine the first quarterfinalists. Morocco made history by defeating Canada 3-0, becoming the first African nation to reach back-to-back World Cup quarterfinals. This victory, however, meant Canada was the first co-host nation to be eliminated from contention. France, led by Golden Boot candidate Kylian Mbappé, secured their spot with a 1-0 win over Paraguay, setting up a highly anticipated rematch of the 2022 World Cup semifinals against Morocco.

Spain also advanced, overcoming Portugal 1-0 in a tight contest, marking their first quarterfinal appearance since winning the tournament in 2010. They are now set to face the winner of the USA-Belgium match. Meanwhile, England secured their third consecutive quarterfinal berth with a 3-2 victory over Mexico. Their next opponent will be Norway, who delivered a significant upset by defeating Brazil 2-1, with Erling Haaland scoring both goals, marking Norway's debut in the World Cup quarterfinals.

The final quarterfinal slot remains open, awaiting the outcomes of the Argentina-Egypt and Switzerland-Colombia Round of 16 clashes. These results will finalize the bracket, promising an exhilarating lineup of matches as the tournament progresses towards its climax.
