The United States Men's National Team concluded its 2026 World Cup group stage play with a hard-fought 2-2 draw against Turkey, a match significantly impacted by the return of star player Christian Pulisic. Entering the game in the 58th minute after missing the previous match due to a calf injury, Pulisic immediately injected a palpable energy and sharpness into the USMNT's attack. His presence not only created several scoring opportunities but also seemed to elevate the performance of his teammates, ultimately helping the squad secure first place in Group D and setting the stage for the knockout rounds.

## What happened
Christian Pulisic made his much-anticipated return to the field in the 58th minute, replacing Tim Weah. Despite his recent calf injury, Pulisic quickly demonstrated his sharpness, getting involved in play, forcing the Turkish goalkeeper Cakir into smart saves, and creating multiple dangerous situations, including a cross that nearly resulted in a goal. His immediate impact was evident as he consistently threatened down the left flank, showcasing his trust in his left leg.

Before Pulisic's entry, Sebastian Berhalter scored a crucial equalizer for the USMNT, bringing the score to 2-2 early in the second half. Berhalter's well-struck, first-time shot through a crowded penalty area capitalized on Turkey's inability to clear the ball from a set piece. His focused celebration, quickly retrieving the ball to restart play, underscored the team's determination to win. Despite some initial struggles for other attacking players like Tim Weah and Gio Reyna to make their mark, the USMNT's resilience ensured they clinched first place in Group D, confirming a Round of 32 match against Bosnia and Herzegovina.

## Why it matters
Pulisic's successful return and immediate impact are critical for the USMNT's aspirations in the knockout stages. His ability to create chances and raise the team's overall attacking level provides a vital offensive spark that will be necessary against tougher opponents. The draw itself, particularly coming from behind, demonstrates the team's resilience and mental fortitude, a crucial attribute for deep tournament runs.

Securing first place in Group D is also strategically important, as it likely positions the USMNT for a more favorable path through the early knockout rounds. While the team showed flashes of brilliance, the initial struggle to involve key attacking players before Pulisic's entry highlights areas for tactical refinement. Overall, the match served as a crucial test and a confidence booster, confirming that their star player is ready for the challenges ahead.
