## How to think about it
Treat Saturday as a day for indoor projects, museum visits, or gear testing under damp conditions. Bring a lightweight rain jacket, waterproof shoes, and protect electronics with dry bags. Reserve Sunday for hikes, bike rides, or patio dining, but still carry sunscreen for the midday sun. Check local alerts before heading out, and adjust plans if rain intensity changes.
