## What happened
Following the "historically beefy" group stage, Alexi Lalas has revealed his full 2026 FIFA World Cup bracket. His boldest prediction sees France facing Colombia in the final, with France ultimately emerging as the champion. Lalas has consistently championed Colombia since before the tournament, feeling validated by their strong performance against Portugal. However, he acknowledges that overcoming France, which he describes as the "most impressive team of the group stage" with deep talent like Kylian Mbappé and Ousmane Dembélé, would be an incredible feat for Colombia.

Regarding the United States Men's National Team, Lalas anticipates they should comfortably win their Round of 32 match after topping their group. The real test, he suggests, will come in the Round of 16 against a team like Senegal or Belgium, which he considers to be on par with or potentially superior to the US. Advancing further into the quarterfinals would likely pit the USMNT against a formidable opponent such as Spain, which Lalas describes as a "hard one" and a "moment in American soccer history" if they were to win. He expressed more confidence in a potential semifinal berth if the US were to face Portugal in the quarterfinals instead of Spain.
