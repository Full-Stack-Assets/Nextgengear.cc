## What happened
The GoPro Hero 13 Black bundle is currently on sale at Amazon for $379.99, marking a new record-low price. This represents a $100 discount from its previous price of $479.99. The camera itself typically retails for $429, making the bundle deal particularly attractive as it includes several valuable additions at a lower overall cost than the camera alone.

The bundle includes the GoPro Hero 13 Black camera, a handle, two Enduro batteries, two curved adhesive mounts, a 64GB SanDisk MicroSD card, and a carrying case. These accessories provide everything needed to start filming immediately, enhancing the overall value proposition of this limited-time offer.

## Why it matters
This price drop is significant for anyone considering a high-end action camera, as the GoPro Hero 13 Black is widely regarded as the industry's leading model. Its 5.3K video resolution, rugged waterproof design, and advanced features like Bluetooth audio support and versatile mounting options make it a favorite among serious creatives and adventurers. The inclusion of two Enduro batteries addresses a common concern for action camera users regarding battery life, allowing for extended shooting sessions.

For social media creators, the 27MP sensor's ability to record footage in an 8:7 aspect ratio is a game-changer, simplifying the process of adapting landscape footage for portrait outputs without awkward cropping. This bundle not only makes a premium device more accessible but also equips users with the necessary tools to maximize its potential right out of the box, just in time for summer adventures.
