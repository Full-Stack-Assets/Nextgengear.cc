This influx of fresh titles aims to captivate a broad audience, from sci-fi aficionados and reality TV devotees to fans of crime thrillers and true stories. For subscribers, this means a wealth of new viewing options to dive into throughout the summer month.

## What happened
July 2026 marks the arrival of several key titles on Paramount Plus, headlined by the fourth season of the original series *Star Trek: Strange New Worlds*. This prequel to the original *Star Trek* series will premiere its 10-episode season on Thursday, July 23, with new episodes dropping weekly. Simultaneously, CBS's long-running reality hit *Big Brother* returns for its 28th season, kicking off with a 90-minute premiere on Thursday, July 9, available live for Paramount Plus Premium subscribers and next day for Essential plans.

The platform is also introducing exclusive original content, including the post-theatrical debut of the crime thriller *Wardriver* on July 8, starring Dane DeHaan as a hacker embroiled in a dangerous cyber heist. Additionally, the three-part docuseries *The Real Wolf of Wall Street* premieres on July 14, featuring interviews and never-before-seen footage that delve deeper into Jordan Belfort's notorious exploits. The detective series *Diarra From Detroit*, originally from BET Plus, will also become available as a Paramount Plus original this month.

## Why it matters
These new additions are crucial for Paramount Plus as it continues to carve out its niche in the streaming market. The return of popular franchises like *Star Trek* and *Big Brother* provides strong anchor content that appeals to established fan bases, driving subscriber engagement and retention. Exclusive originals such as *Wardriver* and *Diarra From Detroit* showcase the platform's investment in fresh storytelling, while *The Real Wolf of Wall Street* taps into the enduring appeal of true crime and biographical narratives.

The diverse genre mix ensures that Paramount Plus caters to a wider demographic, from sci-fi enthusiasts and reality TV bingers to those seeking gritty thrillers or in-depth documentaries. This strategic content refresh helps the service remain competitive against other major streamers, offering compelling reasons for both new and existing subscribers to stay tuned throughout July and beyond.
