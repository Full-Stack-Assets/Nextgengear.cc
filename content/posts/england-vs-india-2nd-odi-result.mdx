England won the 2nd ODI against India by 4 wickets, with Joe Root scoring 99 not out. India's batting lineup posted 233 runs, with Virat Kohli and Shreyas Iyer contributing significant runs. However, England's chase was led by Root's steady batting, supported by other batsmen.

Joe Root's innings was pivotal, marking his 17th unbeaten score of 99 in men's ODIs. His batting was characterized by his ability to soak up pressure and steer England to victory in challenging conditions.
