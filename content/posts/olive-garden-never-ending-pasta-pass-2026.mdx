Olive Garden has announced the return of its iconic Never-Ending Pasta Pass after a six-year hiatus. The pass offers 13 weeks of unlimited pasta, homemade sauces, and protein toppings, plus never-ending soup or salad and breadsticks for $100. 

    <Callout type="takeaway">The Never-Ending Pasta Pass will be available for purchase on July 16, offering 13 weeks of unlimited pasta for $100.</Callout>

    ## What happened
    The Never-Ending Pasta Pass was last offered in 2019 and has been a highly anticipated sale for fans of Olive Garden. The pass includes 13 weeks of unlimited pasta, homemade sauces, and protein toppings, accompanied by never-ending soup or salad and breadsticks. Passholders can enjoy early access to Never-Ending Pasta Bowl starting Aug. 24, and the promotion officially begins Aug. 31 to Nov. 22.

    The sale date for the Pasta Pass is Thursday, July 16 at 2 p.m. ET, and it will be available on PastaPass.com. Since its debut in 2014, Pasta Passes have been claimed within minutes every year they were offered.

    ## Why it matters
    The return of the Never-Ending Pasta Pass is significant for fans of Olive Garden, who have been requesting its return for years. The pass offers a unique experience for pasta lovers, allowing them to enjoy unlimited pasta and other menu items for a fixed price.
