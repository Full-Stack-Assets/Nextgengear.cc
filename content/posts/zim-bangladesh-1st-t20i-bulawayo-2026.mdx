Zimbabwe's bowlers set the tone in the first T20I against Bangladesh at Bulawayo on July 15, 2026. Muzarabani and Ngarava delivered spell‑binding overs that left Bangladesh reeling. Bangladesh responded with a Taskin Ahmed boost, but the under‑strength side struggled to contain the hosts. The result gave Zimbabwe a clear advantage heading into the series.

## What happened
Muzarabani and Ngarava overwhelmed Bangladesh with disciplined line and length, extracting movement off the pitch and forcing several early wickets. Their spell curtailed Bangladesh's top order, limiting any momentum.

Bangladesh introduced Taskin Ahmed to strengthen their attack, but the side remained under‑strength due to injuries, affecting field placements and batting depth. Despite a late surge, they could not chase down the target set by Zimbabwe.
