Fujifilm has announced two new versions of its trusty disposable QuickSnap camera to celebrate its 40th anniversary. The QuickSnap Black and White and the QuickSnap Active offer unique features such as black-and-white film and waterproofing. These cameras provide a fun and easy way to capture memories.

<Callout type="takeaway">Fujifilm's new QuickSnap cameras offer a unique photography experience with black-and-white film and waterproofing.</Callout>

## What happened
Fujifilm has launched two new QuickSnap cameras, the QuickSnap Black and White and the QuickSnap Active. The QuickSnap Black and White features a 27-shot roll of ISO 400 black-and-white negative film and a built-in flash. The QuickSnap Active is a waterproof version of the disposable camera, replacing the current waterproof QuickSnap, and features 27 exposures on its ISO 800 Color negative film.

The QuickSnap Black and White is designed for capturing refined black and white images, while the QuickSnap Active is ideal for use in active scenes such as at the beach or pool. Both cameras will be available in the fall, with the black-and-white camera costing $22.90 and the QuickSnap Active coming in at $24.75.

## Why it matters
The launch of these new QuickSnap cameras is significant as it marks the 40th anniversary of the QuickSnap series. With over 1.7 billion units sold worldwide, the QuickSnap series has been a popular choice for casual photography. The new cameras offer a unique experience with black-and-white film and waterproofing, making them appealing to a wide range of users.
