## What happened
Today, July 4th, marks the beginning of the Round of 16 in the 2026 FIFA World Cup, with 16 countries still in contention. This stage introduces immediate elimination, intensifying every fixture as teams battle to extend their tournament run.

The initial two highly anticipated matchups feature Canada against Morocco, and Paraguay taking on France. For the Canada vs. Morocco game, Canada's lineup includes Maxime Crépeau, Jonathan David, and Tani Oluwaseyi, coached by Jesse Marsch. Morocco counters with Yassine Bono, Achraf Hakimi, and Brahim Díaz, under the guidance of Mohamed Ouahbi.

In the second match, Paraguay's squad features Orlando Gill, Miguel Almirón, and Julio Enciso, led by coach Gustavo Alfaro. France fields a formidable team including Mike Maignan, Kylian Mbappé, and Ousmane Dembelé, managed by Didier Deschamps. This particular match is scheduled to be played in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States.
